# Requirements - Quick Prototype Sprint

## Project: Rider App

I want to create a rider app where one rider can share ride with another rider in the middle of journey and share cost

## Sprint Overview

Quick prototype sprint for generated project structure

## Epics

### Mid-Journey Ride Sharing

Develop a platform for real-time mid-journey ride sharing to maximize ride capacity and reduce costs.

### AI Route Optimization

Implement AI-driven route optimization for improved ride efficiency.

### Partnership and User Growth
